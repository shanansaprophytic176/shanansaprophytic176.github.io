## 🔍 Troubleshooting steps
If the game still refuses to connect, follow these additional steps:

* Check your Firewall
Sometimes Windows Defender blocks the game. Open your Windows Security settings, go to "Firewall & network protection," and ensure Phasmophobia has permission to communicate through both Private and Public networks.

* Update your Network Drivers
Old network drivers often cause connection drops. Open your "Device Manager," find your Network Adapters, and select "Update driver" for your wireless or ethernet card.

* Flush your DNS
Open your Command Prompt by typing "cmd" in the Windows search bar. Right-click it and run as administrator. Type `ipconfig /flushdns` and press Enter. This clears old cache data that might point your game to incorrect server addresses.

* Verify Game Files
Open Steam, go to your Library, right-click Phasmophobia, and choose Properties. Select "Installed Files" and click "Verify integrity of game files." Steam will replace any corrupted game data.
